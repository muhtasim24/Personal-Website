Psi-Beta Autonomous Tank

In a team of 15, the Psi Beta pledge class of Theta Tau Professional Engineering Fraternity built a remote controlled tank with a mounted nerf gun. Attaching a bluetooth module to the motor allowed us to control the tank's movement directions, nerf gun aim directions, and the trigger and reload functionality through a mobile phone

Tech Stack: C++ | Arduino
