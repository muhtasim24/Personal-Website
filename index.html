About Me

Hello, my name is Muhtasim Mushfiq. I recently graduated from the University at Buffalo with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I have honed my technical skills and theoretical knowledge, equipping myself with a solid foundation in software engineering principles, high-level programming languages, and database management. My coursework has provided me with hands-on experience in object-oriented programming, algorithms, data structures, working in teams, and many programming languages such as Python, Java, Scala, C, ARM Assembly, MongoDB, and many others. I am hardworking with a passion for technology, learning, computer science, and problem-solving. My enthusiasm for collaborating and working in teams has been a driving force in my professional journey.

I have completed two software engineering internships focused on creating user-friendly solutions. At CytoCybernetics, I enhanced software that helps electrophysiologists conduct research. At GymRat LLC, I designed features to increase user interaction and app usage. Additionally, as a Project Manager intern at the University at Buffalo, I led a team, managed tasks, and planned sprints for an educational application.

These experiences have not only strengthened my technical skills but also deepened my appreciation for teamwork and collaboration. I thrive in environments where I can continuously learn and tackle new challenges. Furthermore, I am seeking a position where I can further cultivate my expertise, drive innovation, and contribute meaningfully to impactful projects. Thank you!
